# 1. **Travel Super App**

This is the strongest and most modern term.

A **Travel Super App** is a platform that offers all travel-related services in one place.

Examples:

* Trip.com
* Traveloka
* Klook (partially)

Your system fits this category perfectly.

---

# 2. **Integrated Travel & Tourism Platform**

Used when a system provides:

* accommodations
* transportation
* tours
* attractions
* travel services
  All integrated into one digital ecosystem.

This is a very professional term for business, investors, or government partnerships.

---

# 3. **All-in-One Cambodia Travel Marketplace**

This term focuses on:

* marketplace model
* multiple suppliers (hotels, guides, drivers, tour operators)
* multiple services operating inside Cambodia

Excellent name if your platform specializes in Cambodia only.

# 4. **OTA + Tourism Marketplace Hybrid**

Most OTAs (like Booking.com, Agoda) only do hotels.

If you combine OTA + marketplace features, your system becomes a hybrid platform, which includes:

* OTA (booking, hotels, homestays)
* Marketplace (guides, tours, activities, transportation)

This is a powerful position in the travel industry.
